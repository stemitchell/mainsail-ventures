Mainsail Ventures was built to acquire and operate one excellent commercial electrical contractor in the DC, Maryland, and Virginia corridor. Not roll it up. Not flip it. Operate it.

The owner-operator businesses I am talking with were built over decades. The people who built them deserve a buyer who understands what that looks like up close. I live in Gaithersburg. I will be in the field, in the trucks, and in the office every week.

My background is fifteen years of leading operations, HR, and integration work inside federal contracting. I know how to take a small business through change without breaking what makes it good. My approach pairs that operating discipline with a licensed Master Electrician partner, so the trade work stays in trade hands while the business strengthens around it.

If you are an owner thinking about what comes next, or someone who wants to back this kind of acquisition, I would like to talk.

Acquisition Focus

Targeted by geography, trade, and growth lane.

One business. One region. Three secular tailwinds that make commercial electrical the best risk-adjusted trade in the DMV right now.

01 / Geography

Montgomery County and adjacent

Primary search in Montgomery County, with active outreach in Frederick, Howard, and Prince George's counties. Local presence is a real advantage against private equity buyers who fly in for two-hour meetings.

02 / Trade

Commercial electrical contracting

NAICS 238210, $2.5M to $20M revenue, owner-operator businesses where succession is a real question. Service-heavy mix and a diversified customer base preferred. Master Electrician operating partner already in place.

03 / Growth

EV, BEPS, and life sciences

Maryland's Building Energy Performance Standards, the EV charging buildout, and the AstraZeneca and Samsung Biologics expansions in MoCo create a decade-long retrofit and fit-out tailwind for a well-run electrical contractor.
